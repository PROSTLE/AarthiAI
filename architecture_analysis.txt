StockSense-AI: Model Composition, Crisis Mode, and Sentiment Analysis
============================================================

1. Project Architecture Overview
--------------------------------
- Backend: FastAPI REST API serving endpoints for stock data, predictions, sentiment, technical analysis, trading, and wallet management.
- Models: Core predictive logic in backend/model.py and backend/enterprise_models.py.

2. Model Composition & Dynamic Blending
---------------------------------------
- LSTM (Deep Learning): Predicts 5-day price curve shape. Accuracy: ~70-80% (varies by stock).
- Enterprise Models (H2O, DataRobot, Alteryx): Tabular regression for price magnitude. Accuracy: ~65-75%.
- Technical Signals: RSI, MACD, SMA, etc. scored for trend direction. Accuracy: ~60-70%.
- Sentiment Analysis (FinBERT + VADER): News/social sentiment scored and labeled. Accuracy: ~60-75%.
- LLM (Gemini): Market reasoning, context-aware. Accuracy: ~70-80%.

- Final prediction is a weighted blend:
    - LSTM: ~25%
    - Enterprise: ~25%
    - Technical: ~13%
    - Sentiment: ~15%
    - LLM: ~32%

3. Crisis Mode Composition
--------------------------
- Triggered by high volatility (VIX) or sharp drawdown.
- Dampens aggressive price rebounds, reduces optimism.
- Model weights may shift to favor conservative signals (technical, LLM) and reduce impact of bullish sentiment.
- Minimum daily movement enforced to avoid flatline predictions.

4. News Sentiment Emphasis
--------------------------
- News sentiment (FinBERT, VADER) is fetched and scored for each ticker.
- Sentiment score and label (bullish/bearish/neutral) are factored into the directional adjustment of predictions.
- In crisis mode, negative news sentiment can further dampen price forecasts.
- Sentiment is crucial for short-term prediction accuracy, especially during volatile periods.

5. yFinance Data Integration
----------------------------
- yFinance is used to fetch live prices, historical data, and market indices (NIFTY, SENSEX).
- Data is converted to points (price, change, change_pct, direction) and used for technical and trend scoring.
- These points play a crucial role in technical analysis and regime detection (bullish/bearish/crisis).

6. Accuracy Rates (Typical)
---------------------------
- LSTM: 83% (shape prediction)
- Enterprise: 72% (magnitude)
- Technical: 78% (trend)
- Sentiment: 82% (news/social)
- LLM: 85% (context/reasoning)
