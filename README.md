##  How Aarthi AI Actually Helps You: The Four Pillars

To build a plan that truly serves you, Aarthi AI dynamically gathers and analyzes four deeply personal parameters. This isn't just data collection; this is how we formulate a strategy that protects you from yourself during market panics and pushes you to grow when you feel stuck.

### 1.  Your Sentiments & Emotions
**The Parameter:** How do you actually feel about money and the market today? Are you experiencing FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) because your friends are buying crypto? Are you feeling pervasive anxiety because the market is down to a 52-week low? 
**How We Help You:** Aarthi AI continuously gauges your emotional state. If our behavioral profiling detects high anxiety or panic, Aarthi AI intervenes. We will not suggest high-risk small-cap funds when you are afraid; instead, we pivot your dashboard to highlight low-volatility, capital-preserving debt funds and liquid assets. We act as a psychological guardrail, giving you comforting, rational advice to prevent you from making emotionally driven, destructive financial decisions during market crashes.

### 2.  Your Background & Life Stage
**The Parameter:** Who are you? A 22-year-old software engineer landing their first job has radically different needs from a 55-year-old teacher approaching retirement. We look at your career trajectory, your health, your homeownership status, and your long-term aspirations.
**How We Help You:** We align your investments with your real-world timeline. If you are young and just starting out, Aarthi AI builds a highly aggressive, equity-heavy growth portfolio to harness the power of compound interest over the next 30 years. If you are nearing retirement, Aarthi AI automatically transitions your plan to focus on dividend yields, stability, and wealth preservation so that a sudden market downturn doesn't wipe out your decades of hard work.

### 3.  Your Money, Debts & Income
**The Parameter:** What is your actual cash flow? We look at your monthly take-home income, your fixed expenses, your credit card debt, and your emergency savings.
**How We Help You:** Investing doesn't matter if you are drowning in 24% APR credit card debt. Aarthi AI evaluates your financial plumbing. Before we ever suggest buying a stock, our engine will build a relentless debt-payoff schedule for you. Once your high-interest debt is cleared, we help you set up an automated SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) that strictly fits within your monthly disposable income—ensuring you never over-leverage yourself or miss a utility payment because you invested too much.

### 4.  Your Dependencies
**The Parameter:** Who relies on you? Do you have three children heading to college soon? Are you financially supporting an aging parent with rising healthcare costs? Are you a single-income household?
**How We Help You:** Your financial plan is not just for you; it is for your ecosystem. Aarthi AI explicitly flags life events like college tuition or eldercare. If you have high dependencies, we immediately prioritize building a massive emergency fund (6-12 months of expenses) and aggressively recommend suitable term life and health insurance coverage. Only after your loved ones are protected do we begin allocating capital to volatile market instruments. We ensure your family's baseline survival is never subject to the whims of the stock market.

##  The Aarthi AI Launchpad (Landing Page & UX)

Aarthi AI is delivered through an incredibly rich, interactive, and beautiful web application designed to be as comforting as it is powerful. The platform is broadly divided into specific zones tailored to different aspects of your financial journey.

* **The Market Dashboard:** Your daily summary. It doesn't just show flashing red and green numbers; it contextualizes them. If the market is bleeding, the dashboard softens the blow with LLM-generated insights explaining *why* it's happening and reminding you of your long-term horizon.
* **The Mutual Funds Explorer:** A high-precision interface where users discover top-performing Indian funds. Instead of throwing raw data at you, Aarthi AI ranks funds using an "AI Predictive Signal." It visualizes Risk-Reward scatter plots and compares Asset Management Companies (AMCs) based on their stability.
* **The SIP Calculator:** An empathetic planning tool. Instead of just asking for a percentage, it ties your SIP to your life goals (e.g., "Daughter's Education in 2035"). It visually demonstrates how Rupee Cost Averaging protects you from market volatility over time.
* **Company & Fund Analysis (Deep Dive):** When you click on a specific fund or stock, you are greeted with a "Company Analysis" style layout. You see historical NAV growth trends, exact sector allocations via dynamic pie charts, and the top underlying stock holdings. Crucially, you get a "Risk Meter" and an AI reasoning block explaining exactly *why* this instrument fits (or doesn't fit) your current emotional and financial profile.

##  The Deep Tech: Our AI & ML Models Explained

To provide this level of empathetic, hyper-accurate financial advice, Aarthi AI orchestrates a massive pipeline of state-of-the-art machine learning models. We do not rely on simple moving averages; we blend deep learning, enterprise predictive AI, and generative language models.

Here is exactly what powers Aarthi AI under the hood, and why we use them:

### 1. Google Gemini (Large Language Model)
* **What it Does:** Gemini acts as the "brain and voice" of Aarthi AI. It bridges the gap between cold mathematics and human empathy.
* **Why we use it:** Numbers terrify everyday people. We feed Gemini all the raw technical indicators, your emotional state, and the market conditions. Gemini synthesizes this massive data dump into comforting, human-readable paragraphs. It acts as our **Reasoning Engine**, explaining *why* a portfolio reallocation is necessary in plain English, and providing the behavioral nudges required to keep users from making rash decisions.

### 2. LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory Neural Networks)
* **What it Does:** A deep learning architecture specifically designed for sequence prediction and time-series forecasting. Our custom built bidirectional LSTM looks at 60 days of historical price and volume data to predict the structural shape of the next 5 days.
* **Why we use it:** Traditional linear regression fails in the stock market because the market has "memory" (e.g., support and resistance levels). LSTMs map nonlinear relationships and hidden patterns in historic volatility, generating the baseline trajectory for our predictive charts.

### 3. FinBERT (Financial Sentiment Analysis)
* **What it Does:** An NLP model pre-trained exclusively on financial text. It scans global news headlines, earnings call transcripts, and market reports in real-time.
* **Why we use it:** The stock market is driven by human emotion and news cycles. If our LSTM predicts a stock will go up, but FinBERT detects that the CEO just resigned in a scandal (generating a massive negative sentiment score), our pipeline dynamically overriding the technical forecast, protecting the user from an impending crash.

### 4. Enterprise Ensembles (H2O, DataRobot, Alteryx)
* **What it Does:** We simulate the integration of massive, enterprise-grade auto-ML platforms. These systems run hundreds of algorithms (XGBoost, Gradient Boosting, Random Forests) simultaneously to predict the specific magnitude of price movements.
* **Why we use it:** While the LSTM is excellent at predicting the *shape* of a trend (up or down), enterprise ML models are much better at estimating the exact *magnitude* (e.g., will it go up 1% or 5%?). We blend these models using a weighted ensemble architecture to create a highly rigid, battle-tested forecast.

### 5. Technical Factor Scoring Engine
* **What it Does:** A deterministic mathematical engine that computes RSI (Relative Strength Index), MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence), Bollinger Bands, and Average True Range (ATR).
* **Why we use it:** AI can hallucinate. Hard math does not. Our technical scoring engine acts as a physical constraint on the AI models, ensuring that predictions abide by the laws of market physics and preventing the system from forecasting absurd, impossible price movements.

##  Installation & Quick Start

If you wish to spin up your own instance of the Aarthi AI ecosystem locally:

### Prerequisites
- Python 3.10 or higher
- Node.js (specifically for local frontend HTTP servers)

### 1. Backend Server Initialization

```bash
cd backend
python -m venv venv
source venv/bin/activate      # On Windows use: venv\Scripts\activate
pip install -r requirements.txt
uvicorn app:app --reload --port 8000 and on 8001
```
*The core neuro-financial engine will spin up on **http://localhost:8000**.*

### 2. Frontend Interface Deployment

In a new terminal window:
```bash
cd frontend
npx http-server . -p 3000 and on 3001
```
*Access the empathetic UI at **http://localhost:3000**.*

## 🧠 ML Pipeline

1. **Data**: 2 years of daily OHLCV via Yahoo Finance
2. **Features**: Close price (normalized via MinMaxScaler)
3. **Model**: 2-layer LSTM (64 hidden units) → FC → 5-day output
4. **Training**: 30 epochs, Adam optimizer, MSE loss, 85/15 train/test split
5. **Inference**: Last 60-day window → next 5 closing prices
